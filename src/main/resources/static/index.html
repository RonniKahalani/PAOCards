PAO Matrix

Start by memorizing this PAO Matrix which relates a person, an action and an object to every card and groups them into personality pairs (male + female). Only the religious column is single.

Quiz

To boost your training you need a trainer to challenge your current memory performance. This is exactly what the quiz feature offers.

You get a random deck of cards and have to recall the PAO properties for a given card.

Features

  • Auto Reveal - by clicking the checkbox for a PAO property the selection list will show the correct value.
  • Keyboard Shortcuts - one click actions boosts your performance.
  • Pause Timer - allows you to take a break and return to continue the quiz.

Memory Palace

To memorize a full deck of cards you need a system called memory palace.

You're trying to memorize 51 cards, well 52 cards but the last card is self-evident and does not need to be memorized explicitly.

The mental memory palace contains 17 locations each holding 3 cards making up a weird sentence about a person, by combining a PAO property from each of the cards.

You have to transform these location sentences into a memorable mental visualization and transform them back to playing cards.

PAO Matrix Quiz

Are you ready to test your matrix memory?

Learning to master memory palaces and memorize a Person-Action-Object (PAO) matrix might sound like a waist of time, but it will give you the ability to memorize over 17 places, with 3 cards each, in chronological order, which is a cool and valuable skill for your mind.

Getting Started

  • Strive to memorize the matrix associations across cards and suits.
  • Train 15 min. each day,with the quiz and memory palace tools.
  • If memory palaces is new to you, spend time to close your eyes and walk down your memory lane.
  • Practice regularly on improving your memory skills.

Features

  • Ready to use right away or change to better fit your needs.
  • Simple creation of your own matrix-, card and memory palace data.
  • Training quiz that helps you exercise.
  • Keyboard shortcuts to boost your performance.

Suits

The suit of a card is related to a personality type like:

People who are likable.
People who makes you smile weirdly or wrinkle your nose.
People who likes bling and shiny luxury.
People with an itch in the body or brain.

Quiz

  • Use the quiz to train your PAO matrix memory.
  • Use the keyboard shortcuts to speed up your training.
  • Use the timer to track your progress.
  • Use the "Auto Reveal" options to help you learn.
  • Use the "New Deck" button to shuffle the cards and make a new deck.
